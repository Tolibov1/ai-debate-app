# Phase 1 — Scope

- **Topic selection** — Preset topics with optional trending markers.
- **Time limit** — Choose a debate duration before starting.
- **Debate chat** — Back-and-forth messages with the AI opponent.
- **Finish & review** — End the round early or when time runs out.
- **Review breakdown** — Score (1–10), strongest point, weakest point, feedback, and verdict.
